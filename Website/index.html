FAQ

What are the Regular Rates?

At daytime, the guests can accommodate the resort from 7am until 5pm with a rate of P10,000. During nighttime, they can accommodate the resort from 6pm until 6am with a rate of P15,000.

Where to pay down payment?

Please kindly deposit to the bank account of the resort.

Does the resort gives refunds during cancellation?

Yes, they can get refunds only if they informed the resort that they are cancelling their reservation not less than five days of the reserved date.

Can we bring our pets?

Yes, as long as there will be no damages or troubles caused by them. Please watch them for sanity maintenance.

Does your resort has a restaurant?

No, we don’t have but we allow the guests to bring their own foods. We also allow liquors inside the resort.

Does all your facilities included on the accommodation?

Yes you can use all the facilities of the resort. Rooms are also included with the total bill. You can use both Pavilions (Pavilion 1 and Pavilion 2). There will be no additional charges.
